Free Solo is a National Geographic documentary directed by Elizabeth Chai Vasarhelyi and Jimmy Chin. It follows professional climber Alex Honnold as he attempts to free solo climb El Capitan in Yosemite National Park — meaning he climbed without ropes or safety gear. The film captures the beauty of climbing while also showing the risks and emotional challenges that come with chasing something this extreme.
